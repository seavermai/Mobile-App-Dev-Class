Q1. What is the goal of the application?

The goal of the application is to provide a quick and simple checklist application for tasks such as to-do lists and shopping lists.

Q2. Describe your target audience.

My target audience is anyone who would seek to improve or maintain their efficiency and organization through the use of a checklist, right on their mobile device.

Q3. Find at least 3 apps that deal with a similar topic as yours.

It shares similarities with basic apps that take notes, such as the default Android Memo app, the default iPhone Notes app, and the Checklist app by Checklist.com for Android.

Q4. Briefly describe what you like about them.

I've made use often out of both the Notes app on my iPhone 5 and the Memo app on my Samsung Galaxy S5. More recently, I had used the Memo app to keep track of my weight-lifting and fitness progress overtime. I have also looked at the Checklist app, though I haven't personally used it yet. I enjoyed the simplicity of both Notes and Memo. Checklist offers a ton more functionality, such as scheduling reminders and events.

Q5. Describe their weaknesses.

Likewise, the Notes and Memo apps were too simplistic for other needs. As a result, whenever I had a series of tasks or had to go shopping, it was a little frustrating to modify each task as "completed" - I would have to go into the editing feature to manually erase each entry in order to denote that I was done. The Checklist app, while powerful, offered too many extra features that I didn't need, thus increasing the learning curve for using it.

- Simplistic

- Useful
